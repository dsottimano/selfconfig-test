# Writing

Posts and Pages open in a full-screen writing canvas. The big text at the top is
the **title**; below it is the body.

## The slash menu

Type **`/`** on an empty line to insert a block:

- **Headings** (H2, H3), bullet and numbered lists, quote, divider.
- **Callout** — a highlighted box for notes or warnings.
- **Image with caption** — see *Media*.
- **Embed** — paste a URL (e.g. a video) to embed it.

## Formatting

Select text to get a small toolbar: **bold**, *italic*, link, inline code. Standard
shortcuts work too (⌘/Ctrl-B, ⌘/Ctrl-I).

## Settings drawer

The **⚙ Settings** button (top-right) opens a drawer with everything that isn't
the body: excerpt, featured image, categories/tags/author, per-entry SEO, and (for
Pages) content blocks. The **Published** toggle and the title stay in the main
chrome.

## Saving

**Save** commits the entry to your repo. The body is stored as HTML — Lanza is the
source of truth, and the site renders it directly (sanitized). You can save as
often as you like; each save is a commit.
